professional things

After graduating, I worked as a Financial Analyst with BMO Financial Group in Chicago and separately hosted a podcast focused on innovation in the early-stage funding market. This led me to join Krossover, a New York-based sports analytics company, where I came on as Director of Operations. In that position, I spent a majority of my time in Bangalore, India where I led International Operations and eventually oversaw the development of our global freelance sports analyst marketplace. I currently work at seedchange, an early stage investment bank focused on connecting accredited investors with early-stage opportunities.
